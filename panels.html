    Week 1 Assignment: Your Health Context Persona

    • With your partner, come up with a “persona” for a patient with the chronic disease you chose. Please start a team google doc, and in it put down the details of your patient’s story which should include:
      • Demographics (name, age, sex, ethnicity, etc.)
      • A “history of present illness” – what’s the story of how/when this person’s disease started and progressed to this point? What were the first symptoms? What is the current stage or level of symptoms?
      • Some medications that the patient currently takes, with dose, route, frequency
      • A few other clinically relevant details for this patient, e.g.:
        • Allergies
        • Past medical or surgical history
        • Significant family history
        • Social history (alcohol/drug use, smoking etc.)
      • While your persona may not have finished the trajectory of their illness, provide a description of the progression of the disease/health issue as it moves along its course to possible cure or ultimately to its end stage
    • This “Health Context Persona” will be used by you and your partner for the rest of the class homeworks… so thought that you put into it now will be helpful all semester!

    Week 2 Assignment: EHR Exercise

      Use the Practice Fusion EHR to document a SOAP note on your HC Patient.
      SOAP Note: Subjective, Objective, Assessment and Plan:
      • Subjective – Patient’s description of their reason for a visit/chief complaint (in their own words)
      • Objective – Provider’s observation (e.g., measurements e.g., weight, vitals, physical exam findings
      • Assessment (provider’s impression, diagnosis)
      • Plan – Additional tests/treatment plan (e.g. order tests, prescribe meds, outside referrals)
      • Here are a few examples

      Exercise Steps:
      1. Create your account in Practice Fusion. You will get an invite, practice code is: DD77FR5NEC
      2. Register your patient - fill in as much detail as possible and play around with settings
      3. Check in your patient and chart a SOAP Note
        1. Select “charts” on side panel
        2. Select your patient
        3. Select “New Encounter (SOAP)
      4. Be sure to record something in each field
        1. Chief Complaint (why did the patient come to the office today?
        2. Vitals (notice calculated BMI)
        3. Diagnoses (record at least your chronic disease, notice the mapped ICD and SNOMED codes, we will cover in future class)
        4. Allergies (add one! Notice there are a lot of allergy components, including reaction, severity, onset, etc.)
        5. Medications (add a few medications related to your chronic disease, just “Record,” don’t “Order”)
        6. Social History (be creative)
        7. Past Medical History (be creative)
        8. The SOAP elements of the note. Play with the built in templates. You can stop after PLAN of SOAP (don’t bother with orders, attachments, etc.)
      5. Also note!
        1. Check out the clinical decision support items at the top, especially if any are related to your disease.
        2. When writing SOAP, check out the “Add patient info” and template functionality that can make note writing faster. Try using a community template (add one to “My Templates” in settings).
        3. After you save and sign your note, check out the patient summary page and see what data made its way there.
      6. Finishing up
        1. Print a final copy of your note to a PDF and submit with your homework.
        2. Each individual team member should write up some thoughts on the experience - guided by these questions:
          1. What surprised you about using the EHR?
          2. What was great/handy about the system?
          3. What was terrible about the system?
          4. What intrigued you (you’d like to learn more)?
          5. What mystified you (what’s that all about)?
          6. Any other thoughts?
